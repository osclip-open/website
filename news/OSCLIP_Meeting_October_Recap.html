OSCLIP HOLDS FIRST IN-PERSON MEETING

Published November 10, 2025

OSCLIP had its inaugural in-person meeting on October 17, 2025 at Carnegie Mellon University in Pittsburgh, PA, in conjunction with a whole week of action packed open science events at Carnegie Mellon University Libraries. A sense of community was cultivated, lots of challenges were articulated, many ideas to solve these challenges were created. Really appreciate everyone for your commitment and lively contributions to this community we are building together. Thanks to the generous support from Carnegie Mellon University Libraries and colleagues of the Open at CMU team! Please stay tuned on takeaways, project opportunities, and more activities to come!
