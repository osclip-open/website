Announcing Open Science Collaborative of Library and Information Professionals (OSCLIP)!

Published May 1, 2025

We are thrilled to announce the creation of the Open Science Collaborative of Library and Information Professionals (OSCLIP)! This group brings together library and information professionals who share a passion for advancing open science.

OSCLIP aims to connect these professionals, amplify their collective impact, and provide mutual support and resources to strengthen their roles as essential components of the open science ecosystem. Our kickoff meeting will be held virtually on Monday, May 19, 2025, at 1:00 - 2:00 PM EDT.

Inaugural steering committee: Huajin Wang, Meghan Testerman, Sam Teplitzky, @Wasila Dahdul, Melanie Gainey, PhD, Kristen Scotti.

To learn more: https://osf.io/qc53z
